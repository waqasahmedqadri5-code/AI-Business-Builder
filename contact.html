✦ Quick Answers

Common Support Questions

Starter plan users typically hear back within 24 hours. Pro plan users within 4 hours. Business plan users have a dedicated account manager and get responses within 30 minutes during business hours.

Phone support is available exclusively for Business plan users. All other plans use email and live chat. We find that written support creates better documentation and faster resolution for most issues.

Our full documentation, video tutorials, and community forum are available at docs.aisyte.ai. We also have an active Discord community with over 12,000 members where you can get help from fellow builders.

For bugs, use the in-dashboard bug reporter or email support@aisyte.ai. For security vulnerabilities, please use our responsible disclosure program at security@aisyte.ai. We take security reports very seriously and respond within 24 hours.
